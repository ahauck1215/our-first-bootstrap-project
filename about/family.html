Candy Connections is an award winning, small business established by husband and wife duo, Anthony and Daneya Jacobs, in 2014. The family-owned and operated pop up candy shop specializes in freshly made cotton candy and Italian ice. Candy Connections carries unique cotton candy flavors including but not limited to green apple, lemonade, grape soda, and unicorn birthday cake, to name a few. Candy Connections can be found in the Christiana Mall seasonally from April 1st- September 30th.

Candy Connections is passionate about serving the entire family. In 2018, Anthony and Daneya formed a non-profit as an extension of their work with Candy Connections to provide yearly intergenerational family events with intentional spaces to create memorable and joyous moments. The Candy Family, an affectionate name for the Candy Connections owners, understand first hand how busy life can get and the importance of creating sweet memories along the way.
